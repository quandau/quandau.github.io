Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2024

At Climate Today, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app, including your data provided through Google OAuth authentication.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we collect personal information such as:

  • Email address: Provided during the authentication process via Google OAuth.
  • Profile information: We may also collect basic information such as your name and profile photo.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow you to log in to our app using your Google account.
  • Personalization: To tailor the app experience to your account and preferences.
  • Communication: To send you information related to your account or the app’s functionality, if necessary.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with third parties for the following reasons:

  • Google OAuth: By logging in with your Google account, we use Google’s authentication service to verify your identity. Google may collect and store your personal data as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party service providers who help us operate the app, such as for database management, hosting, or email services.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: You may request to view the personal information we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your data: You may update your account information at any time within the app.
  • Delete your data: You may request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at contact@yourapp.com.

6. Cookies

Our app may use cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small files that are placed on your device to help us recognize you and customize your experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the app may not function properly without them.

7. Compliance with Laws

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): For users in the European Union, we adhere to GDPR guidelines on data collection, storage, and user rights.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): For users in California, you have the right to access, delete, and opt-out of the sale of your personal data under the CCPA.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the Effective Date at the top of this policy.
